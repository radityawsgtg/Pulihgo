# 💡 Feature Ideas & Advice

**Friends — this is your space.** Add an idea by copying the template below and
filling it in. No idea is too small. We review these together and decide what fits.

**Before you add:** skim [`docs/01-concept-brief.md`](./docs/01-concept-brief.md)
(boundaries) and [`docs/06-feature-spec.md`](./docs/06-feature-spec.md) (what's
already planned) so we don't duplicate. During the 30-hour sprint, most new ideas
become **Phase 2/3** — that's fine, we capture them here for later.

## How we tag ideas
- 🟢 **MVP** — helps the core gyroscope loop; sprint-worthy
- 🔵 **Phase 2** — adherence/game/library
- 🟣 **Phase 3** — cloud + therapist dashboard
- ⚪ **Parking lot** — good, but out of scope for now
- ❌ **Out of bounds** — conflicts with our boundaries (e.g. diagnosis)

---

## Template (copy this)

```
### <short idea title>
- **By:** <your name>
- **What:** <1–2 sentences: what it does>
- **Why it helps:** <the user/clinical/business benefit>
- **Effort guess:** small / medium / large
- **Suggested tag:** 🟢 / 🔵 / 🟣 / ⚪
```
